Research

My general research interests are in the broad field of artificial intelligence (AI) and statistics. Currently, I am into the intersection of AI and computational engineering, targeting at the development of novel AI methods for modern engineering problems, particularly in the integrated circuit (IC) design and smart manufacturing. My current research focuses on the following topics:

Foundation Machine Learning Techniques for Engineering In General

  1. Multi-Fidelity Fusion

  2. Physics-Enhanced Machine Learning

  3. Spatial-Temporal Field Modeling for Digital Twins

  4. Design Space Exploration and Visualization

AI4EDA (AI for Electronic Design Automation)

  1. Design Space Exploration for analog and mixed-signal circuits

  2. Modern Statistical Methods for Yield Analysis and Optimization

  3. Uncertainty Propagation and Control in EDA and TCAD (Technology Computer-Aided Design)

  4. AI-driven SPICE Simulation Acceleration

  5. AI for Statistical Static Timing Analysis (SSTA)

AI4Science

  1. AI for Smart Scientific Equipments

  2. AI for Density Functional Theory (DFT) and Molecular Dynamics (MD) Simulations
