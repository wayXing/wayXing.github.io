Research

My general research interest are artificial intelligence (AI), where I mainly focus on the sub-fields of data-driven engineering design/optimization and physical simulations. Some of my current research projects are:

  1. Machine learning for design automation

    1. Bayesian optimization

    2. Efficient yield analysis and optimization

    3. SPICE simulation acceleration

    4. Design space exploration and visualization

  1. Machine learning for physics

    1. Surrogate model (for high-dimensional, multi-fidelity, and stochastic systems)

    2. Physics informed machine learning

    3. Bayesian method for dynamical systems

    4. Inverse problems

    5. Digital twins

    6. Reduced order model (ROM) with data-driven methods
